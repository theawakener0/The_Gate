Ahmed Projects

MasteryAI

A standout project in Ahmed's portfolio is MasteryAI, an advanced AI voice agent designed to offer strategic advice, philosophical insights, and psychological guidance. This project showcases his ability to merge cutting-edge artificial intelligence with deep humanistic understanding.


Omar Projects

Assistive Robotics for the Visually Impaired:

Omar is spearheading an innovative robotics project designed specifically for the visually impaired. His work integrates an ARM processor to continuously monitor vital health metrics such as blood pressure, blood sugar levels, heart rate, and blood oxygen levels. This assistive robot not only aids in navigation but also ensures users' safety through real-time health diagnostics.


Ahmed & Omar Projects

The projects led by Ahmed Khaled and Omar Ashraf are a testament to their interdisciplinary approach and forward-thinking mindset. While Ahmed’s projects delve into the realms of artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and educational empowerment, Omar’s initiatives bring innovative robotics solutions to the forefront, addressing critical challenges faced by the visually impaired and other vulnerable groups.


Their work not only highlights their technical prowess but also underscores their shared vision: to create technology that is accessible, impactful, and transformative. Through continuous innovation and a commitment to social good, Ahmed and Omar are paving the way for a future where technology serves as a bridge between advanced computing and real-world human needs.
